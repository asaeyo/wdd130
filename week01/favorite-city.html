My Favourite City: Venice

Growing up I have been intrigued about the city of Venice which I got to know through novels like Merchant of Vernice by Williams Shakespeare and movies watched. It is a beautiful city in Northeastern Italy known for its location in the midst of several islands, with stunning architectural designs. I wish to one day visit the city and behold its unique building designs- notably the St. Mark's Basilica, Doge's Palace, Grand canal, amongst others. I would also love to watch their renowned festivals like the Venice carnival, and film festivals. Hopefully, my dreams will become a reality in the near future.
