White Water Rafting Company

At White Water Rafting Company, we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on the water.

History

We live in a rich tropical rainforest region with the name of our state being derived from the river that defines us. Our vision was borne out of the need to explore the aquatic habitat in our environment in a well packaged manner that encompasses our rich cultural practices, meals and welcoming environment. We look forward to your support as we maximize our fun.
